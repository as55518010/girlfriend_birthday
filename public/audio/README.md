# Audio Assets

Place your audio files here:

## Background Music
- `bg-romantic.mp3` — Main background music (romantic, gentle piano/strings)
  - Recommended: 2–4 minutes, loopable, 128kbps MP3
  - Suggested sources: Pixabay, Free Music Archive, or your own composition

## Sound Effects  
- `sfx-envelope.mp3` — Envelope open sound (soft paper/seal breaking, ~1 second)
  - Recommended: short, subtle, 64–128kbps MP3

## Notes
- Keep files small for fast loading (background music < 3MB ideal)
- The audio player handles fade in/out automatically
- If no files are present, the music toggle still appears but won't produce sound
